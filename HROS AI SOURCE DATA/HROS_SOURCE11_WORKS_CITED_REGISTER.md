PRIMARY SOURCES — SUFFOLK PARISH REGISTERS
================================================================

WP-SFK-008
Wingfield Parish Register. FC84/D1/1. Suffolk Archives.
Contains confirmed primary images:
— John Harper buried 12 April 1546 (Generation 2 —
   proposed son of John Harper Esquire of Epping)
— "John Harper sonne of John Harper" buried
   25 November 1566 (Generation 3 — explicit father/son
   relationship confirmed in primary source)
— Third John Harper buried c.1580 (probable younger
   brother — collateral)
SIGNIFICANCE: Three generations of Harpers buried at
Wingfield, Suffolk, spanning 1546-1580. Combined with
Chancery cases C1/326/40, C1/981/98-99 and C1/1358/21-23,
confirms a documented Harper family operating in Wingfield
and Dennington across a 60-year window. The same individuals
recorded as Harper in legal documents and burial register are
proposed ancestors of the Harbertt/Harberd family at Boxford,
25 miles southwest — the name difference reflecting document
type dependency, not separate families.
[STATUS: CONFIRMED READ — April 2026 | PRIMARY IMAGES
CONFIRMED]
