================================================================
PURPOSE
================================================================
The source index for the entire project. Every document, archive
reference, and secondary source used anywhere in the research
is registered here with a unique WP-ID.

This file contains INDEX ONLY — no interpretation, no conclusions.
Perplexity is the primary maintainer of this file.

================================================================
ID FORMAT
================================================================
  WP-TNA-###    The National Archives documents
  WP-SFK-###    Suffolk Archives documents
  WP-ESX-###    Essex Record Office documents
  WP-DRB-###    Derbyshire Record Office documents
  WP-BL-###     British Library manuscripts
  WP-PUB-###    Published secondary sources
  WP-ONL-###    Online databases (flagged by tier)
