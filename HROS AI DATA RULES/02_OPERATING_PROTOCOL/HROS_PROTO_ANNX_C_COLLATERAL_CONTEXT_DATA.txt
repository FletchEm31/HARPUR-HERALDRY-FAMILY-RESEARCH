ANNEX C — COLLATERAL & CONTEXTUAL DATA
Harper Heraldry & Family Research | Operating Protocol V2.0

================================================================
PURPOSE
================================================================
Background ecosystem mapping. The world the family lived in.
Collateral lines, regional surname clusters, socioeconomic
context, and historical events that frame the research without
being part of the direct lineage argument.

================================================================
WHAT BELONGS HERE
================================================================
  - Unrelated Harper/Harpur lines in the same geographic area
  - Surrounding parish families and witness networks
  - Regional surname clustering data
  - Socioeconomic and historical context documents
  - Collateral gentry lines (Burwell, Chamberlayne, Smythe etc.)
  - Name collision records (documented different individuals
    sharing the same name in the same period)
  - Heraldic context — other Harper/Harpur armorial grants
  - Historical events framing the research period

================================================================
WHAT DOES NOT BELONG HERE
================================================================
  - Direct lineage evidence (→ ANNEX A)
  - Working hypotheses (→ ANNEX B)
  - Probate/wills for direct ancestors (→ ANNEX E)

================================================================
CRITICAL RULE
================================================================
ANNEX C data MUST NEVER be used to support MAIN DOC conclusions
directly. It provides context, not proof. If a collateral figure
turns out to be directly relevant to the chain, move the entry
to ANNEX A via proper promotion protocol.
