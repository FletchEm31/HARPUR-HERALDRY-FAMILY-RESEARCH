ANNEX B — THEORY & HYPOTHESIS LAYER
Harper Heraldry & Family Research | Operating Protocol V2.0

================================================================
PURPOSE
================================================================
The research thinking space. This is the lab, not the courtroom.
Every working hypothesis lives here until primary source evidence
either confirms it (→ ANNEX A / MAIN DOC) or disproves it
(→ retained here with DISPROVEN flag).

A well-maintained ANNEX B demonstrates research integrity to
the College of Arms. Present it openly — it is a strength.

================================================================
MANDATORY LABELS — EVERY ENTRY MUST CARRY ONE
================================================================
  WORKING THEORY      Active hypothesis under investigation
  INFERENCE ONLY      Logical argument without documentary basis
  UNCONFIRMED         Lead exists but primary source not yet found
  DISPROVEN           Investigated and eliminated (do not delete)
  SUSPENDED           Not actively pursued — retained for future

================================================================
WHAT BELONGS HERE
================================================================
  - Hypotheses about unconfirmed generational links
  - Geographic inference arguments
  - Naming pattern analysis without primary source backup
  - Aggregator source data (flagged, not cited as proof)
  - Logical arguments about migration or social continuity
  - Any statement containing: likely / probably / possibly /
    suggests / indicates / may have / could be

================================================================
WHAT DOES NOT BELONG HERE
================================================================
  - Confirmed primary source findings (→ ANNEX A)
  - Collateral family data not related to the main hypothesis
    (→ ANNEX C or D)
  - Random surname clusters with no working hypothesis attached

================================================================
PROMOTION RULE
================================================================
To move from ANNEX B to ANNEX A:
  Must pass PROMOTE_TO_MAIN_DOC validation gate
  Must have minimum 2 independent primary source confirmations
  Must have no conflicting contemporary record

To retain in ANNEX B:
  Single primary source only, OR
  Secondary source only, OR
  Logical argument without documentary confirmation
