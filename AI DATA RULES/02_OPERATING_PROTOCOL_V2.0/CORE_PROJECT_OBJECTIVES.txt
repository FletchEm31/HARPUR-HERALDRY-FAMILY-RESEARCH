CORE PROJECT OBJECTIVES
Harper Heraldry & Family Research | Operating Protocol V2.0

================================================================
OBJECTIVE 1 — LEARN AND RECONNECT
================================================================
Document the Harper / Harpur lineage to understand family history,
cultural roots, and historical context. The story matters.
The truth of the story matters more.

================================================================
OBJECTIVE 2 — DOCUMENT TO SHARING STANDARD
================================================================
Produce documentation that can be shared with family, researchers,
and institutions. Clear. Sourced. Honest about what is confirmed
and what is theory.

================================================================
OBJECTIVE 3 — FIND THE TRUTH
================================================================
Truth above narrative. If the evidence contradicts the story,
the story changes. If the baronetcy connection cannot be proven
to primary source standard, that is the honest result.
We do not build a case on wishful thinking.

================================================================
OBJECTIVE 4 — GRANT OF ARMORIAL BEARINGS
================================================================
Submit a complete, College of Arms-standard lineage to support
a formal Grant of Armorial Bearings from the College of Arms,
London. This requires a documented patrilineal line meeting
their evidentiary standards.

================================================================
OBJECTIVE 5 — DORMANT BARONETCY CLAIM (CONDITIONAL)
================================================================
If primary source evidence confirms the connection to Sir Henry
Harpur, 1st Baronet of Calke Abbey (b.1579), submit a claim
for recognition as holder of the dormant Harpur Baronetcy
(lapsed 1924, never formally declared extinct).

This objective is CONDITIONAL on closing the Essex-Suffolk gap
with primary source documentation.

================================================================
PRIORITY ORDER
================================================================
1. Close the Essex-Suffolk bridge (TNA C1 Bundle 326)
2. Confirm John Harbarde burial/will (Boxford)
3. Confirm Joseph Harberd burial/will
4. Confirm Joseph Harbar × Katherine marriage (Beccles c.1695)
5. Obtain Beccles St Michael primary image Dec 1705
6. Richard Harper III will (Essex probate — highest Essex priority)
7. All items above before any submission preparation begins
