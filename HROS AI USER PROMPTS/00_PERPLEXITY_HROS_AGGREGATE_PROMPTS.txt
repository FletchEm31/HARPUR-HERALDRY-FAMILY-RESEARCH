SIR GEORGE HARPER (1503-1558)
- Born 11 March 1503, ONLY SON of Richard Harper III of Latton, Essex
- Mother: Constance, daughter of Sir Robert Chamberlain of Capel
  and Gedding, Suffolk
- First wife: Lucy, daughter of Thomas Peckham (d.1552)
- Second wife: Audrey, daughter of Sir John Gainsford
- NO legitimate male heirs — putative sons were biologically
  Richard Morison's children
- Made his will 8 November 1558 — NOT intestate
- Died December 1558, buried St Martin's Ludgate, London
- Will reference: PCC 10 Welles — filed as WP-TNA-018, TO ORDER

RICHARD HARPER OF LATTON, ESSEX (father of George)
- Died 10 April 1509
- Son of Richard Harpur of Epping (will PROB 11/9/246, d.1492)
